What I've Learned:

This year I have learned about a whole new side of computers. I have learned, to begin, how to "change" photos. I learned how to use Gimp to make one picture a whole new one! Next, I learned abot 3D modeling. For 3D modeling, I mainly used Blender. I have, honestly, fallen in love with the program. I figured out how to create many things like melting ice letters, a ring, a teddy bear, and so much. I will definetly be continuing with Blender this summer. Another part of 3D modeling was the biosphere project. For this, we used Unreal Engine 4, and that one was really cool. You could set up your creation and then play it. That was really cool. After the 3D modeling unit, we moved on to the coding unit. In this unit, I learned how to code a whole website from scratch! This unit was my favorite next to 3D modeling. I learned how to decide, and insert things into websites from scratch that I coded myself. Overall, I learned so much about computers, that I have never known you could do, or how to do them.
